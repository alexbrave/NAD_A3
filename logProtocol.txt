Log protocol
------------
This is our log protocol that must be followed for the logger to accept a log 
request from a client.

[time in seconds since 1970][pipe]
[instance ID as string of numbers][pipe]
[log level as integer][pipe]
[log message]


Following are the log levels that the logger is compatible with
Log Levels:
-----------
1 - TRACE
2 - ALL
3 - DEBUG
4 - INFO
5 - ERROR
6 - FATAL
7 - OFF
